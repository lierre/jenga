Hi. This is Jenga.

A tool for collective action. We are building a tool to get more people to take action on things that matter.

A tool for an effective citizenry
because being a good citizen
is never enough.

Database of doers

Maintain a database of doers within you community. This will enable communication.

Feedback Loop

Build a real time feedback loop. This will help gather your community thoughts.

Call for action

Call your community to act on the matters that are important to them.
